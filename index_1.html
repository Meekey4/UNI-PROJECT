The remit

Four disciplines, held under one roof, so nothing about you has to leave it.

Privacy & Data

What is known about you

  • Data protection audits. We map where your personal data lives and close the gaps before someone else finds them.
  • Regulatory counsel. GDPR, CCPA and cross-border data law, translated into decisions you can actually make.
  • Digital footprint removal. We locate and retire the records, listings and traces that expose a private life.

Defense & Security

What can reach you

  • Cyber defense advisory. Devices, accounts and households hardened against intrusion and surveillance.
  • Threat intelligence. Quiet monitoring for the early signals that precede an incident.
  • Travel & residence security. Protocols for movement, properties and the people around them.
  • Operational security. The habits and procedures that keep a routine from becoming a vulnerability.

Reputation & Risk

  • Crisis & breach response. A steady hand when something is already wrong — containment first.
  • Litigation support. Discreet evidence and data handling, alongside your counsel.
